# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We are committed to making participation in this project respectful, constructive, and harassment-free for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and professional in discussions
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions
- Give actionable, technical feedback
- Keep security and user safety in mind

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language
- Doxxing, intimidation, or threats
- Deliberate attempts to leak secrets or abuse infrastructure
- Spam, trolling, or bad-faith disruption

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and discussions that violate this code.

## Reporting
To report issues, contact maintainers privately or use repository security reporting channels when sensitive.
